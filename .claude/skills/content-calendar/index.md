# Content Calendar — Editorial Calendar & Content Strategy

## Workflow

### 1. Content Audit
- Inventory existing content
- Analyze performance metrics
- Identify gaps and opportunities

### 2. Content Strategy
- Define content pillars (3-5 themes)
- Map content to buyer journey stages (Awareness → Consideration → Decision)
- Set content mix (educational, inspirational, promotional, community)

### 3. Calendar Creation
- Monthly theme planning
- Weekly content distribution
- Channel-specific scheduling (Blog, LinkedIn, Instagram, Email, YouTube)
- Key dates and events

### 4. Content Template

| Week | Mon | Tue | Wed | Thu | Fri |
|------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 1 | Blog post | LinkedIn | - | Email | Instagram |
| 2 | Case study | LinkedIn | Video | - | Instagram |
| 3 | Blog post | LinkedIn | - | Email | Instagram |
| 4 | Webinar | LinkedIn | Recap | - | Instagram |

### 5. Metrics & Optimization
- Track performance per channel and content type
- Monthly review and optimization
- A/B test content formats and topics

## Usage
```
/content-calendar --brand "Litix" --channels blog,linkedin,email --months 3
```
