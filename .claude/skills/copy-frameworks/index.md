## Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
- **Attention**: Hook that stops the scroll
- **Interest**: Problem/benefit that resonates
- **Desire**: Solution with social proof
- **Action**: Clear CTA with urgency

### PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
- **Problem**: Identify the pain point
- **Agitation**: Amplify the emotional cost of not solving it
- **Solution**: Present your solution as the obvious answer

### BAB (Before, After, Bridge)
- **Before**: Current painful state
- **After**: Desired future state
- **Bridge**: Your product/service as the path

### 4Ps (Promise, Picture, Proof, Push)
- **Promise**: Bold claim or benefit
- **Picture**: Vivid scenario of life with the benefit
- **Proof**: Testimonials, data, case studies
- **Push**: CTA with incentive

## Channel Templates

### Email
- Subject line (40 chars max, curiosity/benefit driven)
- Preview text (90 chars, complement the subject)
- Body (AIDA or PAS structure)
- CTA button text (action verb + benefit)

### Landing Page
- Hero headline (PAS or BAB)
- Sub-headline (clarify the promise)
- Benefits section (3-5 key benefits with icons)
- Social proof (testimonials, logos, stats)
- FAQ section
- Final CTA

### Ad Copy (Meta/Google)
- Primary text (125 chars for Meta, 90 for Google)
- Headline (40 chars)
- Description (90 chars)
- CTA selection
