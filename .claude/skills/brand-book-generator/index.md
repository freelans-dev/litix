# Brand Book Generator — 10 Sections

## Sections

### 1. Brand Story
Origin story, mission, vision, values, brand manifesto

### 2. Logo Usage
Primary logo, variations, minimum sizes, clear space, misuse examples

### 3. Color Palette
Primary colors, secondary colors, accent colors, accessibility ratios, usage guidelines

### 4. Typography
Primary typeface, secondary typeface, hierarchy rules, web fonts, fallbacks

### 5. Photography & Imagery
Photo style guide, illustration style, iconography, image do's and don'ts

### 6. Voice & Tone
Brand voice attributes, tone by context, vocabulary guide, writing examples

### 7. Layout & Grid
Grid system, margins, spacing scale, responsive breakpoints

### 8. Applications
Business cards, letterhead, email signatures, social media templates, presentations

### 9. Digital Guidelines
Website patterns, UI components, animation guidelines, accessibility requirements

### 10. Brand Governance
Approval process, brand guardians, update procedures, violation handling

## Usage
```
/brand-book-generator --brand "Litix" --format pdf
```
