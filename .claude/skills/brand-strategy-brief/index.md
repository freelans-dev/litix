# Brand Strategy Brief — 11 Sections

## Sections

### 1. Brand Overview
Company name, founding story, mission, vision, values

### 2. Target Audience
Demographics, psychographics, pain points, desires, media consumption

### 3. Market Analysis
Industry overview, market size, trends, opportunities

### 4. Competitive Landscape
Direct competitors, indirect competitors, positioning map, gaps

### 5. Brand Positioning
Positioning statement, unique value proposition, Golden Circle (Why/How/What)

### 6. Brand Personality
Brand archetypes, personality traits, tone of voice, do's and don'ts

### 7. Brand Promise
Core promise, proof points, emotional benefits, functional benefits

### 8. Visual Identity Direction
Color psychology, typography direction, imagery style, mood board brief

### 9. Messaging Framework
Tagline options, key messages by audience, elevator pitch variations

### 10. Communication Strategy
Channel strategy, content pillars, campaign themes

### 11. Success Metrics
Brand awareness KPIs, engagement metrics, conversion targets

/brand-strategy-brief --company "Litix" --industry "LegalTech"
